Full range of services:

1. Competence

Each project of FD studio passes through our expert advice, receiving the most correct compositional and stylistic decision. We guarantee that the external aesthetics of the entire project under development will be coordinated and will be supported by technical solutions and technological elements of the object.

2. Personalized services

We value our reputation, so each of our work has an author's idea. And every detail that we put into the project is a well thought out, balanced decision, which we make with the whole team. Our advantage is that we can hear our client. We always offer a sufficient number of options both at the planning stage of the room and at the stage of decorating the space. Everything is always meaningful, has logic.

3. Project economy

Savings in interior design are laid from the start. We guarantee that we will provide maximum protection against errors and alterations. All our project documentation is performed with high accuracy. This is all thanks to the work experience and specialized education of our specialists.
Performing repair work on our drawings, related experts will not ask a single question on the project. They will have a detailed and unambiguous "work card".

4. Implementation

FD studio is ready to undertake all the work of the customer in the organization of construction work.
We are ready to carry out architectural supervision during construction.
We carry out a complete selection of furniture and decor. We complete the object with the use of finishing materials and equipment.
Control at all stages of work is a guarantee of compliance of the project being realized to the idea of interior design.

Every time we get into a new room, which we have to arrange for the customer, we listen to what the space dictates - walls, windows, ceiling height ...

And the space as a result turns out to be correct, undistorted, moderately filled with decor.
