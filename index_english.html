Studio FD - designs residential interiors and corporate premises, taking into account the current directions of modern needs.

Working with you begins with a thorough interview in order to uncover and identify all needs, life and work points of activity without exception. Thus, by combining everything, we will propose appropriate interior solutions, which are the aim of our cooperation.

We set clear priorities: we evaluate room multifunctionality, develop ergonomics, think over space technical possibilities and reality to bring to life all interior developments and solutions. We also assess if reality will meet the conceived project.

We are experts in interior design more than 10 years

Interior design concept

The primary goal is the ideological concept, stylistic solution of all spaces in a unified manner. All this gives to the customer the clear understanding of the design decision.

Full set of drawings

We complete engineering of all design solutions, agreed in the design project. We develop for the builders the necessary assembling and construction units for all the architectural elements of the decor. As a result, this speeds up and provides precise execution of the construction and installation works at the facility. Documentation always contains sufficient project information for the implementation of interior solutions.

Projects Visualization

We project in perspective all previously planned ideas. Having 3D visualizing computer modelling, we can check and analyze what will be the interior before the start of renovation and construction activities. Most importantly is that this allows to finalize the final overall color solution of the spaces, to confirm previously chosen finishing materials, furniture and lighting.

Project realization

Work begins from the moment of the contract execution and can continues until facility commissioning. We provide author's supervision and ready to solve design and implementation issues of any level of complexity.
